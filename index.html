§ 01The PlatformWhat it is

One platform.
Every discipline.
One workflow.

Builtly Engineering is a client portal where geometry, physics, mathematics and AI converge on the complete engineering deliverable — under regulatory authority, with full audit trail. Modules deployed for structural, geotechnical, fire, acoustic, environmental and feasibility analysis. Operating in seven jurisdictions.

For the engineerA

Less paperwork.
More judgment.

One platform for drawing, calculation and analysis. No switching between four tools to ship one report. AI handles the routine production; the engineer focuses on what professional judgment is actually for. Accountability stays where it should — with the person who signs.

For the clientB

Hours, not days.

Delivery on hours instead of days or weeks. Lower cost and faster project velocity throughout. Traceable end-to-end from raw data to signed report. One portal across every discipline — one source of truth for the entire project.

§ 02How it worksFrom project data to quality-assured delivery

The pipeline.

A processing pipeline where each stage is built to do one thing properly. Geometry and physics are deterministic. AI operates under regulatory constraint. Engineering judgment signs. End-to-end traceability — every claim can be rolled back to the source data behind it.

§ 01 · Input
Input
Project files in open formats. The platform accepts every relevant engineering source.
§ 02 · AI
Rules
Multi-engine AI with automatic failover. Models operate within machine-readable regulation.
§ 03 · Physics
Compute
Geometry and statics in pure numerical code. Exact results — never inferred by a language model.
§ 04 · Review
Sign
Professional engineer with regulatory authority signs. Three levels: auto, reviewed, attested.
§ 05 · Output
Output
Final deliverable in open formats. Traceable end-to-end from input data to signature.
