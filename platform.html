Core disciplines.

5 modules
GEO
Geotechnics
Module 01 · Core

Ground conditions, foundations, and Eurocode-7 analysis.

Builtly's geotechnical module ingests soil-survey data, regional geological surveys and topographic information to produce ground-condition assessments, foundation-strategy proposals and Eurocode-7 verification reports. The module handles characteristic value derivation, partial-factor methods and limit-state analyses across the three relevant verification methods.

Used at the early-design stage to set foundation strategy and at the detailed-design stage to verify capacity. Attested by an engineer with regulatory authority in geotechnics.

InputsSoil surveys · DWG · regional geo-data
OutputsPDF report · DOCX · GeoJSON
StandardEurocode 7 · local codes
Typical useFoundation strategy · capacity
Delivery: Attested
STR
Structural
Module 02 · Core

Structural analysis, rationality and dimensioning.

Reads DWG, DXF and IFC4 inputs, runs a structural rationality analysis across the model, applies Eurocode-1 through Eurocode-9 verifications, and produces member-sizing recommendations across steel, concrete, timber and composite construction. Outputs include BIM-native IFC4 models with structural attributes embedded.

Used for both feasibility-stage rationality reviews and detailed-design dimensioning. The output is reviewed by a structural engineer with regulatory authority before release.

InputsDWG · DXF · IFC4 · loads
OutputsIFC4 · DOCX · PDF · BCF
StandardEurocode 1–9 · local codes
Typical useRationality · sizing · BIM export
Delivery: Attested
FIR
Fire safety
Module 03 · Core

Fire-safety concept, compartmentation and egress.

Develops the fire-safety concept document for a building from plan inputs, applies jurisdiction-specific code requirements, calculates compartmentation against fire-resistance ratings, and verifies egress paths against capacity and travel-distance limits. Outputs include both plan markups and the supporting concept report.

Used at concept stage and again at detailed design. Attested by a fire engineer with regulatory authority.

InputsPlan drawings · use class · capacity
OutputsPDF concept · marked-up plans
StandardBuilding regulations · local code
Typical useConcept · compartmentation · egress
Delivery: Attested
ACO
Acoustics
Module 04 · Core

Sound calculations, building and room acoustics.

Computes airborne and impact-sound insulation requirements between dwellings and between functional zones, verifies sound-class compliance across the relevant national standards, and produces room-acoustic predictions for reverberation time and speech intelligibility. Supports both residential and commercial typologies.

Used to verify acoustic design at planning approval and at construction documentation. Attested by an acoustic consultant with regulatory authority.

InputsPlans · construction build-ups
OutputsPDF report · calculation appendix
StandardNational acoustic standards · ISO
Typical useClass verification · room acoustics
Delivery: Attested
ENV
Environmental
Module 05 · Core

Contaminated ground, biodiversity, environmental monitoring.

Assesses environmental conditions for a site — contaminated ground risk, biodiversity exposure, regulatory environmental requirements — and produces the environmental monitoring plan, handling instructions for contaminated material, and the required regulatory submissions. Combines site-survey data with national environmental databases.

Used at site-acquisition due diligence, at planning application, and to govern environmental monitoring through construction. Attested by an environmental consultant.

InputsSite survey · cadastral · ecology data
OutputsPDF plan · monitoring schedule
StandardNational environmental code
Typical useDD · monitoring · regulatory filing

§ 05FoundationsThe architecture beneath

Technical
foundations.

Every module above runs on the same architecture. Six foundations support the entire platform. New modules and new jurisdictions plug in without architectural changes — the foundations carry them.

§ Foundation 01

Hybrid AI architecture.

Hybrid multi-engine architecture combining proprietary engineering models with selectively-invoked Anthropic and OpenAI capabilities. Each request is routed to the engine best suited to the task — proprietary models for engineering-specific reasoning, third-party models for general language and structured extraction. All engines operate under regulatory boundaries; output is gated by the rule pack, not the AI's confidence.

Multi-engine · failover · regulation-locked
§ Foundation 02

Deterministic computation layer.

Geometry, statics and physical calculations run as deterministic numerical code, not as model inference. Eurocode-grade verification, exact-by-default. AI describes and writes up the results; the numbers themselves come from numerical libraries.

Numerical libraries · exact compute · Eurocode
§ Foundation 03

BIM-native processing.

Native handling of IFC4, BCF 2.1 and engineering-format inputs through industry-standard processing libraries. The platform reads BIM as a first-class data structure, not as a re-export from a foreign authoring tool.

IFC4 · BCF 2.1 · ifcopenshell
§ Foundation 04

Geospatial-native systems.

Direct integration with geospatial data, cadastral registers and authority interfaces. Geographic operations are computed in projected coordinate systems with proper handling of national grids and reference frames.

Shapely · GeoJSON · projected CRS · REST
§ Foundation 05

Regulation engine.

Building codes, planning law and engineering standards expressed as machine-readable logic, not free-text instructions to a model. Updates to regulation flow through the engine and propagate across affected modules automatically.

Rule packs · seven jurisdictions · modular
§ Foundation 06

Audit and traceability.

Every claim in a signed deliverable can be traced back to the source data behind it. SHA-256 signing on each version, complete audit trail, deterministic re-runs. Compliance and forensic transparency are properties of the architecture, not features bolted on.

SHA-256 · audit trail · deterministic re-run
