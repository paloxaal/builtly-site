§ 01PrimaryThe customer everything is built for
Engineering consultancies

Built for the consulting profession.

Engineering consultancies are the primary customer for everything Builtly does. The platform was designed from the inside of the profession — by people with regulatory authority across six engineering disciplines — not by software founders looking at the industry from outside.

The proposition is straightforward. Engineers spend the majority of their time on documentation and report production — work that consumes senior capacity without using the professional judgment it actually requires. Builtly is the tool for firms that want to redirect senior capacity to the work that requires it. AI handles the routine layer; the engineer's time goes where it matters.

The result for the consultancy: faster deliveries, more projects per senior, better margin per delivery, and senior capacity finally deployed against work that actually requires it. The result for the engineer: less paperwork, more judgment.

§ 02Adjacent buyersDirect customers of automated modules

Adjacent segments.

Some Builtly modules — particularly the fully automated tier — are bought directly by the parties consultancies traditionally serve. These customers want the underlying analysis without the consulting markup, and the modules are designed to deliver it under regulatory rigour.

Real-estate developers01

Faster feasibility. Faster diligence.

Developers buy automated feasibility studies, technical due diligence and climate-risk reports directly. The use case is decision speed: site acquisition runs on tight timelines, and waiting weeks for a consultancy DD often loses the deal.

Builtly turns multi-week reports into same-day deliverables. For complex sites the developer still engages a consultancy — Builtly accelerates the front end of that engagement.

Banks & lenders02

Technical underwriting at portfolio scale.

Lenders use Builtly for the technical-credit basis behind construction loans, for ongoing construction-loan monitoring, and for portfolio-level climate risk against disclosure requirements. The volume of decisions makes manual consultancy review prohibitive.

The platform produces structured outputs that integrate into credit-decisioning systems and portfolio-monitoring dashboards. White-label and API options available for high-volume buyers.

Portfolio screening, disclosure compliance.

Asset managers and institutional investors use Builtly for portfolio-level technical and climate-risk screening, sustainability-certification pre-assessments, and the structured disclosure outputs required under financial-sector climate-risk regulation.

The platform scales across thousands of assets without scaling expert capacity. Results are auditable and structured for downstream reporting systems.

§ 03Use casesConcrete scenarios across segments

In practice.

Specific scenarios where the platform replaces or compresses work that currently takes a consultancy weeks. The pattern is consistent across segments: a decision that needs technical underpinning, a timeline that does not allow for traditional consulting, and a regulatory standard that the output must satisfy regardless.

  1. 01

    Site acquisition under timeline pressure.

    Real-estate developer

    A developer is bidding on a site with a one-week exclusivity window. The traditional path requires a two-week consultancy feasibility study. Builtly returns the indicative volume, regulatory envelope and climate-risk overlay the same day, with engineer-reviewed depth available within 48 hours if the deal advances.

    From weeks to same-day. Decision moves from inference to evidence before exclusivity expires.

    ModulesARK · TDD · CLI DeliveryAuto + Reviewed
  2. 02

    Construction-loan underwriting at portfolio scale.

    Bank / construction lender

    A lender originates hundreds of construction loans per year. Manual technical review per loan is prohibitive, but skipping it surfaces problems too late. Builtly produces the technical-credit basis and ongoing-monitoring framework automatically per loan, with engineer escalation flagged where complexity warrants.

    Volume that was uneconomic to review manually becomes routine. Risk surfaces earlier in the lending cycle.

    ModulesCRE · CLI · TDD DeliveryAuto · API
  3. 03

    Portfolio climate-risk disclosure.

    Asset manager / institutional investor

    A real-estate fund manager is required to disclose climate-risk exposure across portfolio assets under financial-sector climate-disclosure rules. Builtly produces structured per-asset climate-risk outputs for the entire portfolio, with audit trail and methodology suitable for regulatory submission.

    Portfolio-scale compliance without portfolio-scale consultancy fees. Audit trail intact for regulatory examination.

    ModulesCLI · TDD · SUS DeliveryAuto · API
  4. 04

    Consultancy delivery velocity.

    Engineering consultancy

    A consultancy's geotechnical team has 15 small-to-medium projects in queue, all requiring a similar baseline assessment plus project-specific judgment. Builtly produces the baseline assessment for each within hours, and the engineer-reviewer attests the final output. Throughput multiplies; senior attention concentrates on the judgment, not the production.

    Senior engineer time spent on engineering judgment, not on report formatting. Throughput increases without staffing expansion.

    ModulesGEO · STR · FIR · ACO DeliveryAttested
  5. 05

    Public tender preparation under capacity constraint.

    Public agency · consultancy partner

    A public infrastructure agency has more tender packages to review than internal engineering capacity allows. Builtly produces the structured tender-review documentation per submission, runs the consistency checks across packages, and surfaces deviations for human judgment. The partner consultancy handles attestation.

    Engineering bottleneck inside the agency is unlocked. Procurement cadence accelerates without compromising review quality.

§ 04RoutesHow to engage

Three routes.

Builtly is engaged through three commercial routes depending on the segment, the deal size and the regulatory context. The underlying platform is the same; the wrapping varies.

§ Route 01
Platform licence
For consultancies

The primary engagement model. A consultancy licenses the full platform for use across its engineering teams, with seat-based access for engineers and per-deliverable pricing on outputs. Implementation, jurisdictional configuration and team onboarding included.

Suitable for consultancies with regular deliverable volume across multiple disciplines. The most economic route at scale.

PricingSeat + deliverable
OnboardingSame week
Minimum3 seats · 6 months
§ Route 02
Direct purchase
For end buyers

Per-deliverable purchase directly from the portal. The buyer uploads project inputs, selects the module and delivery tier, and receives the output in their preferred format. No licence, no implementation overhead.

Suitable for developers, lenders and asset managers buying automated-tier deliverables on demand. Engineer-reviewed and attested tiers also available where required.

PricingPer deliverable
OnboardingSame day
MinimumOne deliverable
§ Route 03
Partner integration
For platforms & consortia

API integration or white-label arrangement for partners that embed Builtly capabilities in their own offering — proptech platforms, lender portals, consultancy networks, public-sector consortia. The partner controls the customer experience; Builtly provides the production layer.

Suitable for high-volume integrations and where the platform's outputs are inputs to a broader workflow.

PricingNegotiated · volume
Onboarding6–12 weeks
MinimumAnnual commitment
