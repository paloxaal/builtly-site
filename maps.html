Search & ownership

Any property, any owner.

Search by address, property, owner or company. Live matrikkel, areas, BYA/BRA, title holders and full corporate ownership trees from Kartverket and Brønnøysund — with an open-data owner-risk indicator.

Property deep-dive

Everything on one property.

Open any property to its plot, market value estimate from comparable neighbourhood sales, transaction history, owners, tenants, surrounding area and registered building cases.

Map analysis

Draw, measure, analyse.

Toggle map layers, draw and analyse any area, and measure distances. Risk layers for flood, quick clay, storm surge and ground conditions, plus zoning — in 2D, 3D, map or aerial.

Area reports

Know the market.

Instant area profiles — population, demographics, housing prices versus build cost, and new-build supply with median kr/m², units and room mix. Exportable to PDF.

Tenant radar

Find tenants first.

Find companies that will soon need new premises — before competitors. Ranked on growth, industry and stage, with the reasoning behind every signal and a route to contact.

Plot radar

Find buildable land.

Find undeveloped, buildable plots in any municipality by area, with calculated development potential (BYA) and zoning — clustered on the map and in an exportable table.
