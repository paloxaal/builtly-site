§ 01How we workWhat does not change

Working principles.

Builtly is run as the kind of company an engineer would want to work in. The principles below are not aspirational — they govern how decisions get made.

  1. 01

    Engineering judgment first.

    The discipline that owns the work owns the decision. Engineers run engineering. Software runs software. Neither overrides the other on the other's domain. Disagreements are resolved by talking to the person with the regulatory authority.

  2. 02

    Quality over velocity.

    The platform produces work that humans sign and clients pay for. We move at the speed of getting it right, not at the speed of demos. Shipped code is reviewed by engineers, not by sprint cadence.

  3. 03

    Small, senior, long-running teams.

    We hire experienced people, give them ownership, and keep teams small enough that everyone can hold the whole system in their head. Permanence over rotation. Depth over breadth of org chart.

  4. 04

    Open formats. No proprietary lock.

    Builtly itself is built on open standards: IFC4, BCF, GeoJSON, Eurocode logic. The same applies internally — documents, infrastructure, communication. Nothing locked behind one vendor or one person.

  5. 05

    Remote first. Deliberate in-person.

    The work is digital. The team is distributed. We meet in person several times per year for the kind of work that benefits from a whiteboard and a long table. The rest of the time, we work where we work best.

§ 03The processHow we hire

The process.

Four conversations. No technical hazing. No multi-week take-homes. We know your time is expensive, and so is ours. The process is built to give both sides enough signal to decide well — and nothing more.

§ Step 01
01

Application

Send a CV and a paragraph on why this role and why now. No cover letter. We read everything that comes in within five business days and respond either way.

§ Step 02
02

Conversation

A 45-minute call with one of the principals — about your background, what you want from the next five years, and what Builtly actually is. No surprise interviews.

§ Step 03
03

Technical depth

A working session with someone who would be your colleague — on a real Builtly problem. Two hours, paid, no homework. For engineering roles, this includes regulatory discussion.

§ Step 04
04

Offer

Compensation, ownership and scope, in writing, within five business days of the technical session. References checked in parallel. Start date negotiated to your reality.
